💼 How SkinsVault Makes Money

Transparency matters. Here’s exactly how SkinsVault generates revenue to keep the platform running while remaining affordable and fair for users.

🔐 SafeSwap Escrow Service

We charge a 2% service fee on the value of every completed SafeSwap escrow trade. This covers bot maintenance, fraud prevention, server hosting, and ongoing development.

🚲 Marketplace Trades

Our marketplace includes an integrated commission into its sale prices. In addition, we charge a flat 0.5% selling fee per item sold — one of the lowest rates in the market. Our goal is to keep fees minimal while maintaining a secure and seamless experience.

📈 Bundles & ETF-Style Portfolios

Purchases of skin bundles or ETF-style portfolios help support our platform. It’s an optional way for users to invest in diverse assets while contributing to the ecosystem.
